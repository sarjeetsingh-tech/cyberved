India's First CyberVedManthan Summit

A groundbreaking congregation of the nation's foremost cyber warfare experts, gathering March 19-21, 2025 at UPSIFS, Lucknow. Under the patronage of Defense Minister Rajnath Singh and UP CM Yogi Adityanath, the summit aims to forge new pathways in cyber defense through intensive discourse on cognitive warfare, multilateral legal frameworks, and strategic countermeasures.

  • Military-academic collaboration
  • Advanced technical workshops
  • Policy framework development
  • Real-time defense simulations
