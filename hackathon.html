Introduction

Event Overview

The UPSIFS OSINT Hackathon represents India's pioneering initiative in bringing together the brightest minds from academic institutions to explore and advance the field of Open Source Intelligence (OSINT). This groundbreaking event spans 72 intensive hours of innovation, collaboration, and learning.

Participation Structure

  • Teams comprising exactly 5 members from recognized academic institutions
  • Registration fee: INR 1000 per team
  • Original research paper submission (3500 words)
  • Focus on OSINT methodologies and applications

Vision and Mission

Our vision is to establish India's premier platform for nurturing OSINT talent while creating a sustainable ecosystem that promotes ethical intelligence gathering practices. The mission encompasses developing technical expertise, fostering innovation, and creating real-world impact.

Objectives and Outcomes

Educational Enhancement

The hackathon serves as an intensive learning laboratory where participants immerse themselves in advanced OSINT methodologies, Python programming, and data analysis techniques.

Technical Skill Development

Participants engage in rigorous technical training focused on Python programming for OSINT applications, including web scraping, data analysis, and visualization.

Innovation and Research

Teams are encouraged to explore novel methodologies, develop new tools, and propose creative solutions to real-world intelligence gathering challenges.

Professional Networking

Creating a robust network of OSINT practitioners, industry experts, and academic professionals through structured networking sessions.
